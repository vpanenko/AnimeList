91 Days

91 Days is a 2016 anime series produced by Studio Shuka. It premiered in Japan on July 9, 2016 and concluded on October 1, 2016. Crunchyroll aired the series subbed on its streaming service as it premiered in Japan, while FUNimation Entertainment began airing an English dub of the series on its streaming service FunimationNow on October 23, 2016. The story is set in the fictional form of America during the last years of Prohibition in 1932, where brewed liquor dominates the black market thanks to the Italian Mafia. Angelo Lagusa, a young man whose family was murdered in a Mafia dispute, seeks revenge against the Vanetti Family, particularly its Don, Vincent Vanetti which is the Mafiosi that controls Lawless district, and one of the high ranking members of the Cosa Nostra. After seven years in hiding in the Midwest following the night of the murder, Angelo receives an anonymous letter from a friend of his father's, prompting him to return to Lawless and exact his revenge. Under the name of Avilio Bruno, he begins to infiltrate the Vanetti clan by slowly befriending the Don's son, Nero. However, in this dramatic 91-day story, revenge breeds revenge, and killing may yield drastic consequences. How will Angelo Lagusa and Nero Vanetti fare, in this tragic harvest field that they have only begun to sow?

Main Character

Angelo Lagusa

"That's funny. Watching an ass strut around in a lion's skin."

Angelo Lagusa is the main protagonist of 91 Days. He is an orphan who lost both of his parents and younger brother to the Vanetti Family, having witnessed them being gunned down on his birthday. As a result, he harbors a deep grudge. This grudge is awakened when he receives a mysterious letter in the mail from an anonymous man who claims to be a 'friend of his father'. With this letter in hand, he later earns the trust of the Vanetti family through a series of events and joins them in order to avenge his family by killing those responsible for their deaths.
