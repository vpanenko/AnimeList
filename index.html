First one - Hunter x Hunter

This is a really great anime for people who want to look at the progress of characters from scratch, all the top 3 anime on this site will be about this, Hunter x Hunter is a story about a guy Gon who grew up without a mother and father, with Aunt, dad became a hunter and left a message for little Gon that if he wants to see him, talk, then he must become a professional hunter, one of the best, and then he will come to his goal. Since childhood, Gon dreamed of meeting his dad, so he went to the exam to qualify as a Hunter, where he met his best friend Killua in the future and they continued their adventures together, going through all the difficulties, disappointments, they go to their goal - to become hunters. The anime teaches you not to give up and go towards your goal, but you can find out if Gon managed to find his father by watching the anime. I can't even single out my favorite arc here because they're all literally a work of art, a thrilling anime for fans of the genre and a must see.

Thats why this anime got 10/10 from me

Naruto

Come on who doesnt know naruto, it seems to me that everyone knows this anime, even those who have never watched anime, one of the best, unique anime that presents the main character at first as a little weak boy who isnt capable of anything and for 720 episodes shows his growth and the path to the dream is to become Hokage, the anime is full of motivation and makes even the most demotivated person raise their hands. At first glance, the anime may seem for children, but if you immerse yourself in it completely, you can understand that there are motives that are incomprehensible to children, I personally recommend watching this anime not in dub, but with subtitles in the original voice acting. Watching how from a small weak child who is hated by everyone, having no friends, Naruto turns into the most powerful shinobi, who is honored and loved by the whole village, who has acquired a huge number of friends is very exciting. One can only envy his confidence and spirit, I have never seen such inspired people. There will never be an anime like Naruto for me personally.
