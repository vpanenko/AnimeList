First one - Hunter x Hunter

This is a really great anime for people who want to look at the progress of characters from scratch, all the top 3 anime that will be on this site will be about this, Hunter x Hunter is a story about a guy Gon who grew up without a mother and father, with Aunt, dad became a hunter and left a message for little Gon that if he wants to see him, talk, then he must become a professional hunter, one of the best, and then he will come to his goal. Since childhood, Gon dreamed of meeting his dad, so he went to the exam to qualify as a Hunter, where he met his best friend Killua in the future and they continued their adventures together, going through all the difficulties, disappointments, they go to their goal - to become hunters. The anime teaches you not to give up and go towards your goal, but you can find out if Gon managed to find his father by watching the anime. I can't even single out my favorite arc here because they're all literally a work of art, a thrilling anime for fans of the genre and a must see.

Thats why this anime got 10/10 from me
